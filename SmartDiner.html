Smart Diner

A comprehensive restaurant bill splitter and checker

Even if people don’t openly complain, there is often an underlying resentment amongst diners if they are asked to pay more than their share of a restaurant bill. It’s not ‘penny pinching’, it’s being fair to everyone.

Key Features

  • Accurately divides the bill between the diners based on what they have ordered.
  • Enables the final bill to be checked against what has been ordered by the table.
  • Set local tax rates and gratuity/tip.
  • Set an item to be shared equally amongst all diners or just a selection of diners.
  • Set an item as ‘unit price’ so that the quality can be incremented by a single touch.
  • Supports iPhone Light and Dark modes.

Overview

Before You Dine - Set up your table:

Enter the names of the people sharing the bill and the types of food & drink items they will order from the menu (e.g. appetizer, main, sides, desert, soda, beer ....)

While You Dine - Add the Spend:

Set some food & drink items as 'unit price' so the quantity can be simply incremented, and others as being shared, either across the whole table or just selected diners.

After You Dine - Check & Divide the Bill:

Check the itemised bill against the detailed breakdown in the App.

Enter the tax rate and percentage tip to see what each person owes. There is also an option to add a 'fixed amount' if you want to round the bill or apply a money off coupon.

Instructions

There are four tabs:

Bill: View a detailed breakdown of what has been ordered for the table

Summary: View the total bill and the share to be paid by each diner.

  • Set the local tax rate and percentage tip.
  • Or tap the Fixed Amount if you want to just round the bill (or manually apply a discount)
  • Tap the chevrons (>>) to cycle through different ways of displaying the diner's spend. The fixed amount will show in an alternative color if less than the total spend.

Table: Add the names if the Diners and the Food & Drink (e.g. appetizer, main, dessert, sides, wine, beer etc.) by touching the headers. Then touch any row to add the spend.

More: Change the currency settings or reset the data for a new meal.


Under the Food & Drink screen, Fixed Price allows you to set a unit price where every touch on the diner's name will add one (swipe left to subtract). Shared Item, allows the cost to be shared evenly across the diners. Touch the diner's name to include or exclude from the share. For either, touch the row showing the unit price if this needs to be updated.


A couple of things to note:

  • The Fixed Amount for each diner is based of their proportion of the total spend.
  • The tip is calculated from spend before tax. Some establishments may add this after tax,

Finally, give your iphone a shake at any time of a brief contextual help message.
